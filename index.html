About Me:

Greetings! Dr. Miao Zhang is an Assistant Professor at Computer Science Department, Aalborg University (AAU), Denmark. Miao was a Research Associate at the Machine Learning Group, FIT, Monash University, working with Prof. Gholamreza (Reza) Haffari, and Prof. Xiaojun Chang. He received PhD from FEIT, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia, working with Prof. Steven Su and Dr. Shirui Pan.

Email: miaoz@cs.aau.dk; miaozhang1991@gmail.com;

Research Interests

Recently, he focuses on the following topics, include:

  • Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), Neural Architecture Search, Hyperparameter Optimization

  • Bayesian Optimization, Bayesian Deep Learning

  • Meta Learning, Continual Learning, Multi-task Learning

  • Explainable AI

  • Graph Neural Network
